Absolute Extrema:
  • Must be over a closed interval.
  • Extreme Value Theorem:
  • If f(x) is continuous over a closed interval [a,b], then f(x) has both a maximum and minimum over that interval.

Steps To Find Extrema On A Closed Interval:
  1. Find C.V.
  2. Test C.V. and endpoints of interval
  3. Compare results
  4. Smallest is the min; Largest is the max

Applied Maximum and Minimum Problems (Optimization):
  1. Make An Equation
  2. Find The Derivative
  3. Test Values
