A monotonic sequence is one in which the terms all progress in one direction. Types include:
  • Increasing Sequence: a1 ≤ a2 ≤ a3 ≤ ...
  • Strictly Increasing Sequence: a1 < a2 < a3 < ...
  • Decreasing Sequence: a1 ≥ a2 ≥ a3 ≥ ...
  • Strictly Decreasing Sequence: a1 > a2 > a3 > ...
A sequence that becomes monotonic after a certain point is called eventually increasing/decreasing/etc.

Tests to Determine Whether a Sequence is Monotonic:
  • Subtract an+1 - an
  • Take the ratio of an+1 / an
  • Analyze the derivative of the corresponding function
